School Uniform

The Falcon's Nest Hours

The Uniform Shop is located beside the ASK Auditorium and is open each day during school hours.

Purpose

The purpose of the School Uniform Policy is to encourage our students to experience a greater sense of school identity and belonging, to reduce school clothing costs and to encourage a high level of program participation.

General Requirements
  • All clothing must be loose fitting and modest in appearance.
  • Sleeves must drape over the shoulder onto the upper arm.
  • Mid-riffs must not be showing.
  • Shirts must be appropriately buttoned or closed.

Specific Requirements
Tops
Elementary School students will wear light blue.
Middle School students will sport grey.
High School students will don navy.
Students will be able to wear ASK-branded polo shirts and hoodies. These different colors will help us to easily identify the students at ASK.
Bottoms
Navy blue pants, shorts, skirts that are at or below the knees and blue jeans (jeans should not be ripped or faded). Sweatpants, jeggings and leggings are not acceptable attire for our students.
For Physical Education
A PE Kit featuring navy shorts and a white T-shirt will be made available to all students in Middle and High School.
Shoes & Accessories
Running shoes and dress shoes are acceptable footwear.
Running shoes must be worn on PE days.
Flip flops are not acceptable.
Hats are recommended to be worn during any outdoor activities.
Hats are not permitted in class.
About us

The American School of Kuwait was founded in 1964. It is a privately owned, independent coeducational day school which offers a general academic curriculum for students of all nationalities.
