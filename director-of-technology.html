A Welcome from the Director of Technology

Welcome to the 2016-2017 school year!

It is my pleasure to oversee the Information Technology strategy at the American School of Kuwait. I am working to develop, facilitate, and implement initiatives and programs that will improve student engagement and achievement.

I look forward to working with our school's community on technology integration strategies that will enhance and enrich the educational experience for all students from K-12. The IT department is responsible for providing a reliable network for all users and technical assistance in the areas of hardware, network administration, and database support. Furthermore, we provide curriculum support and professional development for teachers, administrators, and staff.

My goal is to maintain, uphold, and develop meaningful uses of technology for teachers, administrators, and, most importantly, students as we strive to "inspire life-long learners who are empowered to excel in American higher education and the global community as productive, compassionate citizens."

If there is anything I can do to assist with your technical needs please feel free to contact me.

Sincerely,

Mr. Dhanak Abhay
Director of Technology
American School of Kuwait
abhaydhanak@ask.edu.kw

About us

The American School of Kuwait was founded in 1964. It is a privately owned, independent coeducational day school which offers a general academic curriculum for students of all nationalities.
