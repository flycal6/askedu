Tuition Fee Policy

Occasionally, the decision is made by parents to enroll or withdraw their children from ASK mid-year. When this happens, one of the three following things can happen depending on the date of enrollment/withdrawal:

  1. Students enrolling at any point during the first semester must pay the full annual tuition fee.
  2. Students withdrawing at any point during the first semester must pay the full annual tuition fee.
  3. Students who enroll at any point in the second semester must pay 60% of the annual tuition fee.

Payment Schedule

The first semester fee must be paid by September 14, 2016.

The second semester fee must be paid by January 22, 2017.

Tuition Fee Deposit

A tuition fee deposit of KD 100 is required with the enrollment form to reserve a seat in the school. However, payment of a tuition deposit ensures a seat only when all other admission criteria have been met.

Once paid, deposit refunds of KD 50 will be given to withdrawing students if requested prior to July 1, 2016.

About us

The American School of Kuwait was founded in 1964. It is a privately owned, independent coeducational day school which offers a general academic curriculum for students of all nationalities.
