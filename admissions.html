A Welcome from the Head of Admissions

One of the most valuable gifts you can give your child is the gift of an exemplary education, one that opens doors and puts your child on the right path. The American School Of Kuwait has engaged students with a rigorous American curriculum within a safe and nurturing environment.

For more than five decades, ASK students from CDC through 12th grade have benefited from a quality private school experience within a supportive community that gathers around the school's core values. Our challenging academic environment, our competitive athletic program, our variety of offerings in the arts, and diverse extracurricular activities allow students to discover and develop their talents to fulfill their potential.

I hope these pages help you get a sense of life at ASK and prove to be a useful tool in guiding you through the application process. Please feel free to contact the Admissions Office if you have any further questions or inquiries.

Please do not hesitate to contact us if you need further information.

I am looking forward to welcoming you to The American School of Kuwait!

Sincerely,

Mrs. Abeer Safawi
Head of Admissions
American School of Kuwait
admissions@ask.edu.kw

About us

The American School of Kuwait was founded in 1964. It is a privately owned, independent coeducational day school which offers a general academic curriculum for students of all nationalities.
