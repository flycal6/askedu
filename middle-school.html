Middle School Administration

Nancy Abdul Ghafoor, Principal

Welcome to the 2016-2017 school year! I am excited to see all our students back after the long summer break and can't wait to meet our new Grade 6. Let's have an outstanding year!

Nancy Abdulghafoor has been a member of the ASK community for the past 13 years. Prior to being assigned as the Middle School Principal she was MS Assistant Principal, She spent four years as the High School Counselor and two years as the Middle School Counselor working closely with faculty, parents, and students. Nancy holds a master’s degree in Educational Leadership and is a certified counselor from Lehigh University. Before going into education Nancy earned her Bachelor’s Degree in Mass Communication and TV Production. However, Nancy’s passion for humanities steered her towards a career in education which started in the classroom. She began at ASK teaching grades 6 and 7 Social Studies. Nancy is also an ASK Alum having graduated from ASK in 1993.



Timothy Allen, Assistant Principal

I am honored to serve you as the assistant principal at the American School of Kuwait. It is a privilege to be a part of a community where parents, teachers, and students care for each other and build positive relationships that support academic and social growth.

As students transition into Middle School and others prepare to move on, it is my belief that ASK Middle School students enter as learners and exit as leaders. We will challenge each student to grow in his or her academic abilities and continue their preparation to be college-ready.

Tim Allen has been working at the American School of Kuwait for the past six years. Tim has taught in Middle School and High School over the past 15 years. Tim holds a Master’s degree in Education and a Master’s in Educational Technology and Leadership. When not at school, Tim enjoys spending time with his wife and three children.



Mayya Salame, Counselor

Welcome to a new and exciting school year 2016-2017! I feel lucky to have been part of ASK community as a student, parent, teacher and as the middle school counselor. I have a background in psychology, special education and guidance and counseling. Mostly, I am looking forward to working with your child through the fun, yet sometimes challenging middle school years.

My primary responsibility is to help students achieve their social, emotional and academic goals. I also aim at providing your child with a safe and supportive environment in order to achieve his or her goals and reach full potential. I will be available to meet with students individually or in small groups to help them meet their needs. Moreover, I plan to conduct small workshops with students to address topics such as study skills, conflict resolution, test anxiety as well as peer relations.

I believe in each student’s intrinsic worth and I am committed to being an advocate for you and your child. Please feel free to reach out at any time throughout the year with questions or concerns.



About us

The American School of Kuwait was founded in 1964. It is a privately owned, independent coeducational day school which offers a general academic curriculum for students of all nationalities.
